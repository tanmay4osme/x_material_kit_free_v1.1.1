About Me

Currently, I am an MBA student at the Technische Universität Bergakademie Freiberg prior to which I was working with Tega Industries Ltd as Senior Engineer-Research and Development based out of Kolkata. I have done my Post Graduation in Mineral Engineering from Council of Scientific and Industrial Research-Institute of Minerals and Material Technology,Bhubaneswar and B.Tech in Mining Engineering from Government College of Engineering(Formerly Orissa School of Mining Engineering),Keonjhar. I have more than 4 years of working experience with product development and research in the mineral processing industry. • Knowledge of troubleshooting in process industries for vibrating screens, crushers, and other equipment. • Knowledge of applications of automation in mining and mineral processing industries and experience building software applications for these systems. • Knowledge of procedures for patenting and research & development. Specialties: Product Development Patent Mining QFD-Quality function deployment ISQ- Inventive System Questionnaire Project Management Team Management Process Audit Process Flow development. I am a change agent and problem solver with a passion for technology, skilled in grasping the big picture, conceptualizing, developing and implementing solutions. Excel in developing and motivating highly focused teams that successfully meet company’s objective. On a personal note, ranging from cooking to travelling, I have a wide range of interests that fascinate my mind & soul. I have many creepy theories of my own regarding life which make my life even more exhilarating. “An ultimate optimist”-This is what I always have to say about myself.
